Our Story

A Reliable Multi-Service Business Serving Ghana

DAFENOVA VENTURES is a sole proprietorship based in Kasoa, Ghana. With approximately ten years of business experience, the enterprise has developed around everyday customer needs — getting projects completed, obtaining essential materials, organising events, printing important materials and moving goods efficiently.

Rather than operating as separate unrelated activities, we bring these services together under one dependable business identity, enabling customers to work with a familiar service provider across several needs.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with DAFENOVA VENTURES.

What services does DAFENOVA VENTURES provide? +
We provide building and road construction, transportation and delivery, electrical product retail and installation, event planning, stationery supply and printing services — all from one accessible business in Kasoa, Ghana.
Where is DAFENOVA VENTURES located? +
We are based in Kasoa, Ghana (GhanaPost GPS: CG-1575-6450). Service coverage for each request can be confirmed when you contact us.
How do I request a quotation? +
You can complete the quotation request form on this page, call us directly on 0531572345, or send a WhatsApp message with the service required, your location and relevant details.
Can you handle more than one service for a project? +
Yes. Customers can discuss combined needs such as event planning with printing and delivery, or construction with electrical supply and installation. We coordinate resources across our service divisions.
What details do you need for event planning? +
Please include the event type, proposed date, venue or preferred location, expected number of guests, the services you require and a budget range where available. Our team will take it from there.
Can I order electrical items for delivery? +
Availability and delivery arrangements can be discussed once you provide the required items, quantities and delivery location. Product availability and specifications are confirmed at quotation stage.
Do you undertake electrical installation? +
Electrical installation requests can be discussed according to the work needed, location and applicable safety requirements. Appropriate, competent personnel are assigned to all installation work.
Can I submit photos or documents with my request? +
Yes. You can send photos, site drawings, item lists, artwork or reference documents directly via WhatsApp to 0531572345. This helps us understand your requirement and provide a more accurate quotation.
