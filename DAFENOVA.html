Who We Are

DAFENOVA VENTURES is a sole proprietorship based in Kasoa, Ghana. With approximately ten years of business experience, the enterprise has grown around everyday customer needs: getting projects completed, obtaining essential materials, organising events, printing important materials, securing stationery supplies and moving goods efficiently.

"Rather than operating as separate activities, DAFENOVA VENTURES brings these services under one dependable business identity."

This approach enables customers to work with a familiar service provider across several needs, while allowing the business to coordinate resources, suppliers and service delivery effectively. Whether you are a household, a business, an institution or a project client, our goal is to make the process easier from start to finish.

We are committed to serving customers with dependable support, careful coordination and practical solutions. Our reputation is built on reliability, integrity and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction.

Direction
Our Mission

To provide dependable, practical and customer-focused services in construction, electrical works, events, printing, supplies and delivery, while building long-term relationships through reliability and responsible service delivery.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and recognised multi-service enterprise in Kasoa and surrounding areas, known for dependable service, good customer relations and steady business growth.
